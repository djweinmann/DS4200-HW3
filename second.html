Rock Climbing

I got into bouldering the summer of 2022, right before I was headed to London for my first semester of college. It felt like a good way to stay active after I was done with running and two of my friends started at the same time so I had a good group to go with. I loved it right off the bat and continued doing it when I was abroad, and now have been climbing consistently for over two years! I’ve met many of my closest friends through climbing, even one of my roommates and my girlfriend. I love how it doesn’t just challenge my body, but each individual climb is a puzzle that I need to solve, and there can be many different solutions that work for different people. So far I’ve only ever climbed indoors in gyms, but I’m excited to get the chance to go and explore some outdoor climbs this coming spring and summer. As much as I do love climbing, it does have some definite downsides. It’s a big time commitment having to travel to the gym and back, and I’ve faced multiple injuries due to overwork in my arms. Despite this, I intend to keep climbing for a long time and love it as a hobby.
